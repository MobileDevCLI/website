## After Filing: What to Expect

### Timeline:

1. **Filing Receipt** - Immediately after submission
2. **Serial Number Assigned** - Within 24 hours
3. **Examination** - 3-4 months after filing
4. **Office Action** (if issues) - Response due within 6 months
5. **Publication** - 30-day opposition period
6. **Registration** - If no opposition, ~8-10 months total

### Potential Office Actions:

1. **Likelihood of Confusion** - Similar mark exists
2. **Merely Descriptive** - Mark describes the goods/services
3. **Specimen Issues** - Specimen doesn't show proper use
4. **Identification Issues** - Goods/services description needs clarification

---

## Trademark Protection Strategy

### Immediate Actions:

1. **Use ™ Symbol** - You can use ™ immediately (no registration required)
   - "MobileCLI™"
   - Shows you claim trademark rights

2. **Document Use** - Keep records of:
   - First use date
   - Continuous use evidence
   - Sales/revenue under the mark
   - Advertising under the mark

### After Registration:

1. **Use ® Symbol** - Only after registration is granted
   - "MobileCLI®"
   - Federal registration provides enhanced protection

2. **Monitor for Infringement** - Set up Google Alerts for:
   - "MobileCLI"
   - "Mobile CLI"
   - Similar variations

3. **Maintenance Filings:**
   - **Section 8 Declaration** - Between 5th and 6th year
   - **Section 9 Renewal** - Every 10 years

## Filing Instructions

### Step 1: Create USPTO.gov Account
1. Go to https://www.uspto.gov/
2. Create an account (free)
3. Verify email

### Step 2: Start Application
1. Log in to your account
2. Go to Trademarks → Apply Online (TEAS)
3. Select "TEAS Plus" (lowest fee option)
4. Choose "New Application"

### Step 3: Complete Form
1. Enter applicant information (Section 1 above)
2. Enter mark information (Section 2 above)
3. Select classes and enter descriptions (Section 3 above)
4. Select filing basis (Section 4 above)
5. Upload specimens (Section 5 above)
6. Review and sign declaration (Section 6 above)

### Step 4: Pay and Submit
1. Review entire application
2. Pay filing fee ($500 for both classes)
3. Submit application
4. Save confirmation and serial number
