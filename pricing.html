Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need my own API key?

Not necessarily! Our main approach uses CLI tools like Claude Code, which bypass traditional APIs entirely. You get better rates, higher performance, and no API key management. However, if you prefer using APIs directly, you can bring your own key.

CLI vs API - What's the difference?

CLI (Command Line Interface) tools like Claude Code connect directly to the AI service with your account login - no API keys needed. APIs require managing keys and have different rate limits. We focus on CLI because it's simpler and more powerful for development.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Cancel anytime and you'll keep full access until your current billing period ends. No partial refunds - you simply won't be charged again. Simple and fair.

What happens when I cancel?

Your subscription runs until the end of the period you paid for. After that, you'll be downgraded to the Free plan automatically. No surprise charges, no hassle. Powered by Stripe for secure, reliable billing.

Is there a free trial?

Yes! Pro comes with a 14-day free trial. No credit card required to start.

What payment methods do you accept?

All major credit cards via Stripe. Team plans can be invoiced. Your payment info is never stored on our servers - Stripe handles everything securely.

Can I switch plans?

Upgrade anytime - you'll be charged the new rate starting with your next billing cycle. Downgrade anytime - your current plan runs until the period ends, then switches to the new plan.

What's included in "Premium templates"?

Pre-configured project starters for web apps, APIs, bots, and more - all optimized for mobile development with Claude Code.
