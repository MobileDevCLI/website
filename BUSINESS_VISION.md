## The Core Innovation

A self-modifying AI development stack:

```
Phone runs App → App runs AI → AI modifies App → Better App runs better AI
```

This is a closed loop that doesn't exist anywhere else.

---

## Use Cases

### 1. Mobile-First Developers
- No laptop needed. Full development environment in your pocket
- Build apps on the bus, in bed, anywhere
- Deploy directly from phone to Play Store
- **Target market:** Millions of developers in countries where phones are primary computers

### 2. AI-Powered App Factory
- Tell AI what app you want → AI builds it → You publish it
- Non-coders can create real Android apps
- Iterate by voice: "Add a dark mode" → Done
- **Target market:** Entrepreneurs, small businesses, content creators

### 3. Self-Healing Software
- App crashes? AI diagnoses and patches itself
- User reports bug → AI fixes it → Pushes update
- No developer intervention needed
- **Target market:** Enterprise, IoT, unmanned systems

### 4. Education Revolution
- Students learn coding by talking to AI inside the app they're building
- See changes instantly on the same device
- No setup, no IDE, no laptop required
- **Target market:** Schools, bootcamps, developing nations

### 5. Rapid Prototyping
- Client meeting: "I want an app that does X"
- Build it live in front of them on your phone
- They see it, give feedback, you iterate in real-time
- **Target market:** Freelancers, agencies, consultants

### 6. Autonomous Agents
- AI runs 24/7 on phone doing tasks
- Monitors things, takes actions, sends alerts
- Can modify its own behavior based on results
- **Target market:** Traders, researchers, automation enthusiasts
